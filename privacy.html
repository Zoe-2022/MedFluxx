Privacy and Security

Understand how we protect your data

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At MedFluxx, safeguarding your personal information is our top priority. We are dedicated to transparency in how we collect, use, and protect your data.

Data We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, contact details, and medical preferences to ensure personalized services.
  • Order Information: Details about your medication orders for seamless processing.
  • Usage Data: Insights on how you interact with our platform to enhance your experience.

How We Use Your Data

  • To process medication orders and manage subscriptions.
  • To provide personalized services tailored to your healthcare needs.
  • For internal analytics and platform improvements.

Data Protection Measures

  • Encryption: Your data is encrypted during transmission and storage.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to sensitive data.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security checks to maintain compliance and protect against threats.

Your Privacy Rights

You can access, update, or delete your personal data anytime. You may also opt out of marketing communications or file complaints regarding data usage or security concerns.

Third-Party Sharing

We only share your data with trusted partners and solely for fulfilling your orders and ensuring service quality.

Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy or the security of your data, please reach out via our Contact Page.
