Frequently Asked Questions

What is Medfluxx?
Medfluxx is a digital health platform designed to provide users with comprehensive healthcare services, including virtual consultations, AI-powered symptom checking, and personalized health coaching.
How does Medfluxx work?
Medfluxx connects users with licensed healthcare professionals, uses AI to assess symptoms, and provides personalized health plans. It’s accessible via mobile, web, and USSD platforms.
Who can use Medfluxx?
Anyone interested in managing their health can use Medfluxx, whether for preventive care, symptom tracking, or consulting medical professionals.
What services does Medfluxx offer?
Medfluxx offers virtual consultations, AI-powered symptom checking, holistic health management, personal health plans, secure messaging, and appointment scheduling.
How do I book a virtual consultation?
Log in to your Medfluxx account, navigate to the "Consultation" section, select a professional, choose an available slot, and confirm your appointment.
What is the AI-powered symptom checker?
It is a tool that analyzes symptoms you input and suggests potential causes or the next steps for medical attention.
How do I reset my password?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your registered email, and follow the instructions in the email to reset your password.
What should I do if I encounter a technical issue?
Contact our support team via the contact form or email us at health@medfluxx.com. You can also access the Help Center for troubleshooting guides.
How does Medfluxx protect my data?
We use advanced encryption and secure servers to ensure your data is safe. Your privacy is our priority, and we comply with all data protection laws.
Are my medical records secure on Medfluxx?
Yes, your medical records are stored securely and only accessible to you and authorized professionals.
Is Medfluxx free to use?
Medfluxx offers a free basic plan with access to essential services. Premium features are available through subscription plans.
How do I upgrade to a premium plan?
Log in to your account, navigate to "Subscriptions," select the premium plan that suits your needs, and complete the payment process.
